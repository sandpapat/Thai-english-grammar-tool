Isolated Classifier Testing

This evaluation tests only the XLM-RoBERTa classifier in isolation, focusing purely on its ability to classify Thai sentences into 24 fine-grained tense categories. No translation or explanation components are involved.

Dataset Characteristics

The test dataset consists primarily of simple sentences rather than compound or complex sentences. This means the classifier was trained and tested on straightforward Thai sentences with single main clauses, which helps achieve high accuracy by avoiding the ambiguity that can arise from multiple tenses within compound-complex structures.
